Overview & Trust

Welcome to Tallmx, a premium health tracker application designed to help you log habits, monitor height growth journeys, and build routines. We respect your privacy and value the trust you place in us when using our application.

This policy details how Tallmx collects, stores, and handles your information. By installing and using the app, you agree to the terms outlined in this document.

Information We Collect

Tallmx functions primarily as a tracker. To provide customization, we allow you to enter the following parameters:

  • Profile Details: Nickname, age, and gender identity to personalize screen greetings and dashboard logs.
  • Height Records: Log records containing your starting height, current height, target height goals, and dates entered.
  • Habit Checklists: Records of routines completed (e.g. stretching schedules, decompression workouts, sleep logs, and water metrics).
  • Daily Metric Logs: Sleep durations logged (hours) and water intake counts (glasses).

100% Offline Storage Model

100% Offline / Local Storage

Your data never leaves your device. Unlike online applications, Tallmx is built using a secure 100% offline data architecture:

  • All statistics, metrics, and logs are kept inside a secure local database (Android Room SQLite) residing exclusively in the app's isolated private sandbox directory.
  • No internet permissions are requested, and we do not maintain external web servers or databases to store user records.
  • Because your data stays strictly on your local device storage, we are physically unable to view, sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers, third parties, or researchers.

Your Rights & Data Control

Since your information is completely local, you have absolute authority and total control over your records:

  • Data Correction: You can edit height entries, units, age, gender, and habits at any time inside the Home or Settings screens.
  • Data Erasure: You can permanently erase all records instantly by using the "Reset Progress Data" option inside the Settings screen or by clearing the app's cache and data through the Android System Settings.
  • Uninstalling: Deleting the Tallmx application from your phone automatically and permanently purges all database records associated with the application.

Third-Party SDKs & Analytics

We believe in keeping our application clutter-free and private:

  • We do not integrate third-party ad networks, telemetry trackers, or analytic frameworks (like Firebase Analytics, Mixpanel, or Google Analytics).
  • The app runs entirely offline, eliminating network leakage risks or unauthorized tracking.

Children's Privacy

Because our application collects zero personal information over the internet and keeps all records stored securely on the local device, Tallmx does not collect, request, or solicit any personal information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and learn that your child has entered information into the app, you can completely erase it at any time by clearing the application's data or uninstalling the app.
